Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to file ITR?+
PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16 (if salaried), bank statements, investment proof, interest documents. For businesses: P&L and balance sheet.
How long does GST registration take?+
Typically 3-7 working days after submission of documents, depending on verification timelines.
Can you represent in court for legal cases?+
Yes ‚Äî our partner advocates provide representation in civil & criminal matters across many jurisdictions. Contact us for location-specific availability.
How do I file an insurance claim?+
We help prepare claim documents, submit claims and follow up with insurers. Use 'File a claim' to get started.
What documents are required for a loan application?+
ID, address proof, income proof, bank statements, KYC and property documents (for home loans). Specifics vary by lender.

Yes ‚Äî we offer retainer packages covering tax & legal advisory. Contact sales for a tailored plan.

What are your service coverage areas?+
Pan-India coverage with remote delivery. For in-person representation we use our partner network in major cities.
How do I cancel a service or request a refund?+
Refunds depend on service terms and work completed. Contact support for assistance and dispute resolution.
